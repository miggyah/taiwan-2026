🔄 Flexible Taipei — Plan B / extra time options
If you finish early, change your mind, or want spontaneous detours, here's a curated list of nearby alternatives (all reachable via MRT or short bus).
📍 Within 30 min from city center (easy to add)
🎨 Treasure Hill Artist Village — riverside squat turned creative commune, galleries & quirky stairs (free, MRT Gongguan + 10min walk). Perfect if you finish Huashan early.
🌳 Daan Forest Park — Taipei's Central Park, tranquil ponds & jogging trails. Great for afternoon break after Yongkang Street.
📖 National Taiwan University campus — sprawling green space, colonial architecture, cheap student cafes. Combine with Gongguan night market.
🛍️ Wufenpu Fashion District — wholesale clothes alleys near Raohe Night Market. If Raohe feels too short, spend an extra hour bargain hunting.
🌿 Northern coast & mountain alternates
🚠 Maokong Gondola & tea houses — instead of Yangmingshan on a hazy day, take MRT to Taipei Zoo and ride gondola ($2) for stunning city panoramas & free hiking trails.
🏞️ Wulai Atayal village — hot springs, waterfall, indigenous culture. 1h bus from Xindian MRT. Perfect if Jiufen feels too crowded (weekend alternative).
🏖️ Fulong Beach & Caoling Tunnel — golden sand beach + old railway tunnel bike ride. Train from Taipei to Fulong (~$5). Great for sunny afternoon instead of Yehliu.
🚲 Keelung Night Market & Harbor — after Jiufen/Shifen, extend to Keelung's Miaokou Night Market (seafood-focused, different vibe). Bus from Ruifang.
🌆 Last-minute / evening wildcards
🎑 Ximending pedestrian zone — only if you crave youth energy & street performances. Best for late snacks after 9PM.
🌉 Rainbow Bridge & Raohe night market extended — riverside walk after Raohe, beautifully lit.
🪕 Dalongdong Baoan Temple — UNESCO candidate temple with stunning night lighting (close to Yuanshan MRT).
💡 Pro tip: If you finish Day 2 early, go straight to Dadaocheng for sunset + Ningxia Night Market combo. For Day 4, if Yangmingshan seems foggy, pivot to Maokong + Zhinan Temple hike — equally stunning.
